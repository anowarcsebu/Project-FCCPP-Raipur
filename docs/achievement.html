Major achievements from the previous phase of the projects

  • Increased the capacity of vulnerable communities living in cyclone prone areas through development of disaster risk reduction infrastructures.
  • Reduce number of death cases comparing to previous cyclone and other disasters period
  • Reduced damage of assets as Early Warning message get in time from Task Forces, Cyclone Preparedness Program (CPP), Media and Mosque Before Cyclone or any other disaster;
  • Through cluster meeting community people increased awareness and highly motivated on practice of WASH, techniques of DRR, preparation for pre, post and during disaster period, water purification through chlorine, hygiene, nutrition etc.
  • Women active participation and mobility at social events and development activities were remarkable comparing to three year back due to different projects implementation.
  • Trained members of DMC at union level and ward level have good knowledge on DM act and SoD on their roles and responsibilities in disaster management.
  • Willingly and active participation was observed among local community during implementation of project activities.
  • The project personnel played a vital role to come out from superstition of COVID-19 and having 100% vaccination
  • A total of 36 WDMCs have been reactivated at 36 wards of the targeted four union under three upazila of Barishal, Chattograma and Khulna Region with the help of respective UDMC.
  • The UDMC of the targeted four unions have four RRAP at Union level and 36 wards have 36 RRAPs. The RRAP of Union level have been approved by respective UzDMC while ward level RRAPs have been approved by respective UDMC.
  • UDMC and WDMCs have reviewed RRAPs every year and implement DRR actions according to the RRAPs.
